⚠️ RULES
PREDICTION RULES
🚫 No AI assistance — Using AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude etc.) to generate your predictions is strictly not allowed. Your predictions must be your own.
🤝 No sharing predictions — Do not tell other players what you have predicted before kick-off. Predictions are personal — keep them secret until the game starts.
⏰ Submit before kick-off — Predictions must be submitted before the match begins. The system will automatically lock predictions at kick-off. Late submissions will not count.
🔒 No changing after kick-off — Once a match has kicked off your prediction is final. Any attempts to alter it will be rejected by the system.
HOW POINTS WORK

GENERAL CONDUCT
⛔ 3 strikes and you're out — If caught cheating you will receive a strike. 3 strikes results in a 5-game ban from earning points. Strikes and reasons are logged by the admin.
⚖️ Admin decision is final — Any disputes regarding scores or points will be resolved by the admin. Their decision is final and binding.
😄 Play in good spirit — This is a family game. Have fun, respect each other and enjoy the World Cup together!
Violating the rules may result in point deductions at admin discretion.
